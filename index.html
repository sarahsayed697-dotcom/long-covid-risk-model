Model methodology
Logistic regression core with coefficients derived from published literature (UK Biobank, ZOE COVID Study, OpenSAFELY, REACT-2). Variant-specific odds ratios applied. Vaccination reduces baseline risk 30–85% by dose count and time elapsed. Symptom burden score contributes a weighted post-acute component.

Formula: P(Long COVID) = σ(β₀ + Σ βᵢXᵢ + γ·SymptomScore), clamped to [2%, 95%]
